# Solana Usage

## Summary

Solana is the core ledger for sigints.club. Signals, streams, and subscriptions are anchored on-chain to provide authenticity, accountability, and transparent ownership.

## How Solana Is Used

- Streams: stream registration and ownership live on-chain.
- Subscriptions: access is represented by on-chain subscriptions, creating verifiable rights to signal access.
- Signal records: signal metadata is recorded on-chain to preserve integrity and auditability.

## Why It Matters

Keeping the protocol on Solana makes the system verifiable. Users and listeners can trust that streams are real, access rules are enforced, and signal records are immutable. This is the foundation for credible signals and accountable publishers.
